WELCOME TO SOJOURNER

The paths which lead me here, and the life and memories left behind.

Hi there

I'm a sojoruner

I set out on a journey alone, leaving behind people and memories in my hometown of Palakkad, India. I traveled by road to Kochi, where I boarded a flight on Oman Airways and ventured through time zones and over the clouds to Muscat Airport. From there, I caught another flight on Lufthansa from Muscat to Frankfurt Airport in Germany and finally, an Air Canada flight from Frankfurt to Toronto Pearson International Airport in Canada. A long and tiring, but exciting journey filled with new experiences and memories. The journey was not just about the physical distance but also about leaving behind the past and embracing new experiences and opportunities.
